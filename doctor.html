Dr. Joshua Sully, DMD, PA

"Patients are my number one priority and having them be in and out in a timely fashion is in both of our best interests. I am determined to create the best dentist office in all of Florida and will continue to. I am able to use the gifts that i have been given to improve the daily lives of so many people and I will continue to do so."

Our doctor belives in making every visit a good visit. Making sure that everyone is happy when they leave is his number one priority and everyone believes that. After studying dentistry for the past 18 years, he continues to improve just how much he has learnt and coming up with the most efficient ways to help patients is on top of the list.

College and Spare time

After going to college for 8 years at the University of Colorado, Dr. Sully continues to improve on what he has already learnt and spends around 6 hours a day teaching students the art of dentistry. In his spare time he enjoys kickboxing and racket ball. He has 4 children that he spends time with daily and works with the community often.
